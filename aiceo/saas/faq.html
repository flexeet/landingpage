Popular Questions

How does Grounded Logic V4.0 work? expand_more
Grounded Logic V4.0 is our proprietary reasoning engine that connects real-time market data with specialized business personas. It doesn't just "chat"—it cross-references inventory levels, ad spending, and historical sales trends to provide actionable strategic bullet points.
Can I train bots with my own internal company documents? expand_more
Absolutely. You can upload PDFs, CSVs, or provide URLs in the Knowledge Base section (coming soon in AICEO War Room). The AI uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to ensure all answers stay within the context of your specific business rules.
How are tokens/credits calculated? expand_more
Credits are spent based on the complexity of the query and the bot's processing depth. Standard chats usually cost 0.5 - 1.2 credits, while deep strategic analyses involving large dataset crunching may cost more. You can track your "Credit Velocity" in the Credits dashboard.
Is my business data shared with other users? expand_more
Never. AICEO uses isolated "Decision Units" (DUs). Each DU is an encrypted sandbox. Your data is used exclusively to fine-tune your own bots and is not accessible to other AICEO users or used for training our global base models.

Still have questions?

Our human support team is available 24/7 for Enterprise users.
